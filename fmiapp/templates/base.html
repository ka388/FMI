Potato Cold Store

Ambient cooling keeps potatoes fresh for up to 3 months post-harvest. This process helps harden the skin and delay deterioration, ensuring quality.

Fruits Cold Storage

Cold storage is ideal for preserving perishable fruits. With the right temperature and humidity, freshness is retained between production and sale.

Tomato Cold Storage

Tomatoes stored above 40°F prevent breakdown. Mature green tomatoes ripen well in controlled environments—ensuring freshness for retailers and farmers.

Onion Cold Storage

Onions are best stored at 25–30°C with 65–70% relative humidity. Proper ventilation keeps them dry, fresh, and durable in varying conditions.
